Snow Tubing is now closed for the season.
Thank you to everyone who joined us this winter. We look forward to welcoming you back next season.

Ski School youth programs, including Traxx, Snow Kamp, and the Learn to Ride program, go on sale starting November 1, 2025. Programs run from December 6, 2025, through March 8, 2026. Registrations made outside of these dates are available on a walk-up basis at 8 a.m., first-come, first-served.

Our season pass sale will begin Wednesday, October 1, 2025. Tickets and rentals will be available for purchase on October 15.

From October 1 through October 31, 2025, you can secure your Unlimited Season Pass for just $500. This pass provides unlimited access to the slopes all winter long, giving you the freedom to ski or ride whenever you choose throughout the entire season. Don’t miss this limited-time opportunity to lock in the best deal of the year and enjoy a full season on the slopes.

Beech Mountain Resort is now closed for the winter season. Thank you to everyone who joined us on the mountain this year and helped make it such a great season.

Scenic lift rides, mountain biking, disc golf, live music, and more return when we open for the summer season on Saturday, May 23.

Ticket prices, hours of operation, and programming will be released soon. Stay tuned.
