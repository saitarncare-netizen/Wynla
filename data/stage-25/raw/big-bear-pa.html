Ski Big Bear Pennsylvania

Located in Northeastern Pennsylvania, Ski Big Bear offers 18 trails, 7 lifts including 3 Magic Carpet lifts, and 650' of vertical. When Mother Nature isn't sending snow our way, our snowmakers make sure the trails are blanketed with as much snow as possible. Our trails offer something for every ability.
