    WELCOME TO BEAR VALLEY.

    Nestled off scenic highway 4 in the Central Sierra Mountains and Stanislaus National Forest, Bear Valley Mountain Resort offers world-class terrain at 8,495 feet. Easily accessible from all of Northern and Central California, Bear Valley is a retreat for both beginner and expert skiers and snowboarders seeking space and breathtaking views. At Bear Valley, you'll find a big mountain ski and ride experience with small town vibes.
