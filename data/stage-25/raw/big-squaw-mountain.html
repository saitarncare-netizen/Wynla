Cat Skiing the iconic 2,950’ summit of Big Moose is a thing! When natural snow dumps we run service for 24 passengers every hour from the top of the triple chair lift. Rides are $30 for season pass holders, $40 for day Ticket holders and $50 for site-seers.

The two mile decent is on groomed trails for all levels and ability. East Branch, Penobscot, Moose River, Piscataquis of St Criox, each offers a panoramic vista of Moosehead Lake, Katahdin and the surrounding mountains. Cat Ski the View!!

About Us

About Us

Our locally run, 501c(3) non profit ski area serves citizens and visitors to Maine’s Piscataquis County. Big Moose Mountain is located 5 miles north of Greenville overlooking magnificent Moosehead Lake.

Executive Director, Amy Lane

President, Rodney Folsom Sr

Vice President, Kevin Springer

Treasurer, Kathleen Moneghan 

Secretary, Allison Economy

Director, Margarita Contreri


Facing northeast not only provides us with the best views in the East, our natural snowpack is well preserved. We offer lift service on a triple chair to the lower mountain and a beginner conveyor lift for our newest skiers. Nordic skiers will find a 9K figure 8 system. Back county is open for uphill adventures too. Come SKI THE VIEW!
