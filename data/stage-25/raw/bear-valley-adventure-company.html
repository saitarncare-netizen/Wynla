Featured Activities

Boat Rentals & Delivery

Boat rentals and delivery services are available to get you paddling on the scenic High Sierra Lake Alpine, Utica, Union, and Spicer Reservoirs.

Mt. Bike Shop & Rentals

Bear Valley is home to an extensive single-track mountain biking trail system, and we have a shop that services and rents mountain bikes.

Reba’s Toll House Cafe

All adventures should include a good meal. Therefore, our cafe is ready to serve you lunch before, during, or after your outing. Cheers!
