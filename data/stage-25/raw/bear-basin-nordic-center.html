      Nordic Ski School

      Our Instructors have extensive backgrounds in cross-country skiing, from coaching to racing, and have spent decades instructing skiers in good technique. We provide a friendly and positive environment for learning and are passionate about improving your skiing. Whether your goal is to race or explore new winter fitness opportunities, our skilled instructors will get you there. Our instructors are PSIA-AASI certified.

      Types of Lessons:

      SIGN UP

      Classic Cross-Country

      Classic Skiing is great technique for beginners and advanced skiers alike. Our instructors will help you get moving or gliding with ease and elegance.

      Skate Skiing

      Skate Skiing is a fun way to fly over the trails. Bear Basin instructors can help you get moving efficiently, honing your balance and technique at any level.

      Private Lessons

      Private one-on-one and group lessons are available for all ability levels throughout the season. Get personalized instruction during these 75-minute private sessions (for you and up to three other members). Take your technique to the next level with a bundle of private lessons.  Lessons must be reserved in advance. Or really hone your skills and establish community with one of our many skill or level-specific clinics.Instructor requests are available.Trail passes are included on the day of your lesson.
