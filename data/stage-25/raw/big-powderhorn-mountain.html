Real memories are created Up North 

Big Powderhorn Mountain Resort is tucked away in the Gogebic Range of Michigan’s beautiful Upper Peninsula. Your escape to the Northwoods begins with 17 feet of average natural snowfall, renowned grooming, family friendly specials, and a down-home staff who treats you like family.

Lake Superior Lake Effect snowfall provides the natural snow that keeps skiers and snowboarders coming back to Big Powderhorn again and again. Throw in a charming Bavarian style ski village and you have the makings for an unforgettable winter getaway in the Upper Peninsula!
