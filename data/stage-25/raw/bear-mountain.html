    Guest Safety & Mountain Awareness Reminder

    Ride in control, follow posted signs, respect closures, and be aware of others around you. These guidelines help keep everyone safe and having fun on the mountain. Check thedaily mountain report before arriving to understand current conditions and available terrain.

    On-Hill Policies Community Guidelines Collision Safety Ski California

    Guests who do not follow mountain safety rules may have their lift ticket or season pass revoked without refund and may lose the privilege of visiting any of our resorts in the future.

    Care For Big Bear
    Help Keep Big Bear Lake Beautiful
    We love our mountain home — and we know you do too. That’s why we’re proud to support the Care For Big Bear campaign. Together, we can keep Big Bear beautiful by showing respect for our environment, our community, and each other.

    Visiting? Please help us keep our local home clean by packing out your trash, respecting wildlife, and staying on trails. Let’s leave Big Bear better than we found it.
