E-1 — Decision Fan (3 branches) ⭐ recommended

Reads as: You start at the bottom dot. Travel up to the decision moment (gold ring). From there, 3 options fan UPWARD only (no backward, no crossing). The middle one in BOLD GOLD with a peak triangle = "the chosen path with destination". The other 2 stay visible but faded = "alternatives we considered".

Why it works for the feedback: All motion is forward (up). Gold thickness + brightness + peak = clearly "this is the right one". The gold ring at the fork marks the decision moment explicitly.
